Healthzoid

Healthzoid is 8-in-1 multi-disease detection system. It supports prediction for brain tumour, breast cancer, alzheimer, diabetes, pneumonia, heart disease, and Parkinson's disease. It is a machine learning, deep learning enabled medical system. It has the potential to decrease the ongoing activity to doctors for prediction of a disease.

PGLife

At PGLife, our mission is to make it easy for students to find their perfect PG accommodation. With our website, students can search for PGs in their desired location and see a list of available options, complete with prices and amenities. We work with a network of trusted PG providers to ensure that our users have access to the best possible options at the best possible prices.

Box-Office

Box-Office is a comprehensive movie search engine that helps users find the movies they are looking for, along with detailed information about each film. With our website, users can search for movies by title, actor, or director, and see a list of results that includes the movie's IDMB rating, release date, and a summary of the plot.
